About the Platform

Your Sales Pipeline,
Now Powered by WhatsApp

In today’s fast-paced logistics and trade industries, maintaining strong, personalized relationships with freight forwarders, importers, exporters, and agents is the key to closing deals. Yet, most businesses struggle with static spreadsheets and fragmented communication.

TargetPing changes that. It is a purpose-built customer list manager with integrated WhatsApp Business broadcasting, designed to supercharge marketing and sales for shipping companies, NVOCCs, and logistics providers. Start by uploading your contact list via Excel in seconds, with every customer structured and tagged by country, sector, and commodity.

The real power lies in precision: filter your database by any attribute, then instantly broadcast rich media campaigns with interactive callback buttons. By logging every delivery and response, TargetPing serves as a lightweight communication cockpit that transforms a static customer directory into a live, high-converting sales pipeline — all powered by the world’s most popular messaging channel.
